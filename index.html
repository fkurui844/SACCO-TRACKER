ℹ ABOUT MATATUHUB ▾
Built for Kenya's
Matatu Industry.
MatatuHub is a modern fleet management platform designed specifically for Kenyan SACCOs and PSV operators. It gives sacco owners full visibility of their fleet — tracking every trip, every fare, every driver — all in real time from any device. No paperwork. No spreadsheets. No guessing.
🚌
REAL-TIME TRACKING
Monitor all vehicles and trips as they happen — live updates across your entire fleet
💰
REVENUE REPORTS
Daily, weekly and monthly earnings per car, per driver — export to PDF or Excel
👤
DRIVER MANAGEMENT
Register drivers, set targets, track performance and send invitations by email
⛽
FUEL & EFFICIENCY
Log refuels, track km/L efficiency and control operating costs per vehicle
🔒 YOUR DATA IS SAFE WITH US
✅
Your data belongs to you. All records are stored securely in a private, encrypted cloud database — no one else can access your sacco's information.
✅
Encrypted accounts. Every user account is protected with industry-standard authentication security — your password is never stored in plain text.
✅
Role-based access. Drivers only see their own trips and stats. Admins control who can see what. No cross-account data leakage.
✅
Works offline. MatatuHub keeps working even without internet — trips are queued and synced automatically when connectivity returns.
